Section 142.
 
    Being  member  of  unlawful  assembly.—Whoever,  being  aware  of  facts  which  render  any assembly  an  unlawful  assembly, 
 intentionally  joins  that  assembly,  or  continues  in  it,  is  said  to  be  a member  of  an unlawful  assembly. 
Section 143. 
  
    Punishment.—Whoever  is  a  member  of  an  unlawful  assembly,  
 shall  be  punished  with imprisonment  of  either  description for  a  term  which may  extend  to six  months,
 or  with  fine, or  with  both.
