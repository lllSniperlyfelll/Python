Section 339.  
         
Wrongful  restraint.Whoever  voluntarily  obstructs  any  person  so  as  to  prevent  that  person from  proceeding  in any 
direction in  which  that  person  has  a  right  to  proceed, 
is  said  wrongfully  to restrain that  person. 


Section 341.  
         
Punishment  for  wrongful  restraint.Whoever  wrongfully  restrains  any  person  shall  
be punished  with  simple  imprisonment  for  a  term  which  may  extend  to  one  month,  
or  with  fine  which  may extend to  five hundred rupees, or  with both.
