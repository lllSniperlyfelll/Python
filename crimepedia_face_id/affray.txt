Section 159.

        Affray.When two or  more persons, by  fighting  in a public place, disturb  the public peace, they are said to  commit  an affray.
 Section 160.
 
       Punishment  for  committing  affray.Whoever  commits  an  affray,  
   shall  be  punished  with imprisonment  of  either  description  for  a  term  which  may  extend  to  one  month,
   or  with  fine  which  may extend to  one hundred rupees, or  with both.
