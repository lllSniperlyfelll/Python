Section 146.  
      
Rioting.—Whenever  force  or  violence  is  used  by  an  unlawful  assembly,  or  by  any  member thereof, 
in  prosecution  of  the  common  object  of  such  assembly,  every  member  of  such  assembly  is  guilty of  the offence  of  rioting.

 
Section 147.  
      
Punishment  for  rioting.—Whoever  is  guilty  of  rioting,  shall  be  punished  with  imprisonment  of either  description  for  a term  which may  extend  to two years,  
or  with  fine, or  with both.
