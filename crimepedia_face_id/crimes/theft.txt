Section 378.  
         
Theft.—Whoever,  intending  to  take  dishonestly  any  movable  property  out  of  the  possession  of any  person  without  that  person's  consent,  
moves  that  property  in  order  to  such  taking,  is  said  to  commit theft. 


Section 379.  
        
Punishment  for  theft.—Whoever  commits  theft  shall  be  punished  with  imprisonment  of  either description for  a  term  which may  extend  to  three years, 
or  with  fine, or  with  both.
